What We Do

Multimedia Creativity Lab is dedicated to pioneer innovative AI systems that empower human creativity and societal progress. Multimedia Studio is dedicated to create inspiring and touching content of humans with AI. They are led by Prof. Anyi Rao at HKUST.

Trustworthy Agentic AI

Prioritize transparency, steerability, and robustness to build trustworthy multiagent systems through AI orchestration that users can understand and control.

AI for Creativity

Invent tools and algorithms to expand artistic expression, blending human ingenuity with AI-generated art, film, music, and narratives.

Human-AI Collaboration

Develop intuitive interfaces and ethical frameworks for seamless human-AI co-creation, ensuring human agency and skill enhancement.

AI for Good

Drive global impact through inclusive, socially responsible AI applications in healthcare, education, sustainability, and underserved communities.
